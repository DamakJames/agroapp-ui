Fertilizer Guide

Coconut
Week - 4

Fertilizer Category: Coconut (Min)

Ratio: 12 : 09 : 32

How to apply:

1. Fertilizer should be applied in a circular pattern, 1 foot away from the base of the plant in the first 1-1.5 years.

2. Distance of application from the base of the tree should gradually increase with growth.
